💪 Daily Wellness Reminders

🥤 Stay Hydrated

Drink water throughout the day! A healthy body needs plenty of water to function properly.

🌳 Outdoor Time

Spend time in nature and play outside. Fresh air and sunshine are great for your body and mind!

🧘 Be Mindful

Take deep breaths and notice the present moment. Mindfulness helps reduce stress and anxiety.

😴 Quality Sleep

Get 8-10 hours of sleep every night. Sleep helps your body grow and recharge!
